by Clifford Patterson

My design was influenced by the themes of Halloween. At the time, I had an interest in ties, and how they looked on people. I thought that making a tie come to life would be interesting, but the way it is presented had to be my main focus. Since we are close to Halloween, I thought of an alien-like grotesque tie showing signs it is alive, but it is not disturbing for people to enjoy the animation.
