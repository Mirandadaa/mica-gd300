Yas Kween & Out of the Closet and Into the Streets

by Daniel Martin-Minnich

Yas Kween: I wanted type to be the focus of both of my gifs. This animation utilizes a hand-drawn style typeface and a comical and celebratory phrase that originated in the House Ballroom scene of New York City in the '80s.

Out of the Closet and Into the Streets: This animation uses a chant from the LGBTQ activist group Queer Nation. Queer Nation was formed in 1990 in New York City to address the escalation of anti-gay and lesbian violence on the streets and prejudice in the arts and media. The original Queer Nation logo had a punk aesthetic which I wanted to incorporate into my animation.
